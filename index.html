Survive Count Doracula's riichi dungeon here in Stockholm, October 25th 2025

Tempted by everlasting glory, creatures from all over find themselves captured in Count Doracula's dungeon. Many will fail and become eternalised in his dead walls, but if you are up to the task and make it through Doracula's demands, you will forever earn your claim to the title of The Dead Wall champion 2025.

Count Doracula's demands

Riichi Mahjong
Witnessing agony at the hands of dora is a must for Count Doracula. Therefore, the participants must partake in riichi mahjong heavily modified to suit his needs.

Costume Contest
Only the most suitable souls end up in Doracula's dead walls. There must be a judgement of character and soul.

Moupai Contest
Nothing marinates the soul better than the deep running anxiety and stress from a failed moupai.

Mahjong Rules: Modified WRC 2025 Rules + Rulecards

The modifications are:
Red fives, Nagashi Mangan(Counts as a win), Counted yakuman and Uma 30/10

Rulecards:
Immediately before the hanchan starts, 3 rulecards are randomly drawn that applies for the duration of that round wind(3 new random cards are drawn at South 1). It is a modification of the Dreamland Odyssey mode released to Mahjong Souls. Familiarize yourself with the details of all the rules here.

Costume Contest: Anyone attending the event can enter the costume contest, including non-players. It is not mandatory. The winner will get a prize and 10000 points counted towards the final score of the entire event. The received points are halved each subsequent place down, stopping at 1000 points. Every costume contest participant will receive points.The voting will be done by all event participants and is anonymous. More in-depth details here.

Moupai Contest: Anyone attending the event can enter the moupai contest, including non-players. It is not mandatory. Best of 1 knockout tournament until the final two. The winner will get a prize and 10000 points counted towards the final score of the entire event. The received points are halved each subsequent place down, stopping at 1000 points. Every moupai contest participant will receive points. More in-depth details here.
