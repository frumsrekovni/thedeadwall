Event Details

The Dead Wall 2025 is a halloween themed one-day event focusing on but not limited to mahjong related activities. Anyone is allowed to attend the event.

There is two registration options: playing and non-playing. Non-playing attendance costs less. With playing registrants there is an expectation that you have played basic riichi mahjong before.

Date: October 25th, 2025

Registration: Opens October 1st

Registration fee: Playing fee is 200 SEK/150 CNY. Non-playing fee is 80 SEK/60 CNY. Both includes food, snacks and drinks. The registration fee is non-refundable after October 20th, 2025.

Payment: Payment is done by Swish or Wechat. See here for QR-codes.

Max participants: No max for attending non-playing attendees. The maximum playing attendees is 36 players, where having some experience playing basic riichi mahjong is expected. Other than that, anyone can register as playing.

Dress code: Halloween attire is mandatory.

Food & Drinks: Food, snacks and drinks are available at your own discretion throughout the event. Alcohol consumption is allowed within moderation, but, alcohol will not be sold at the venue. Bringing your own snacks and drinks is allowed.

Contact: count.doracula@gmail.com

Mahjong games: Modified WRC 2025 Rules + Rulecards. More details here.

Contests: Non-mandatory contests that everyone can join, including non-playing attendees. Each winner will get a prize and 10000 points counted towards the final score of the entire event. Every participant will receive some points. Read more here:
Costume contest | Moupai contest.

The Dead Wall Champion 2025: The attendee with the largest total sum of points from the riichi games, costume contest and moupai contest will be crowned The Dead Wall Champion 2025.
