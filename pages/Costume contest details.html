The Dead Wall 2025
Costume contest details

Anyone attending the event can enter the costume contest, including non-players. It is not mandatory. The winner will get a prize and 10000 points counted towards the final score of the entire event. The received points are halved each subsequent place down, stopping at 1000 points. Every costume contest participant will receive points. The voting will be done by all event participants and is anonymous.

How it will be performed:
Each participant will walk down a runway in front of all event attendees one by one. No longer than half a minute. Accompanied by music and commentary. When everyone has walked the runway the voting begins. The voting criterias are entirely arbitrary and is up to each indivdual voter to make their reasoning. The judging does not have to be solely based on the runway walk but instead imagine it being a culmination of everything up til this point of the event. Each voter is allowed to vote up to 3 different participants. You can only cast max 1 vote on each contestant. On a piece of paper the names or numbers of each contestant is written down and then placed in the ballot box. The most total votes wins.
