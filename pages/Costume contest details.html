The Dead Wall 2025
Costume contest details

The costume contest is an opportunity for you to show off and flex your soul. Anyone attending the event can enter, including non-playing attendees. Show up in costume, any costume, and walk down a short runway in front of all event attendees. That's it!

The costume contest is not mandatory. The winner will get a prize and 10000 points counted towards the final score of the entire event. Every costume contest participant will receive points.

Costume contest rules:
- All event attendees are allowed to vote, including non-playing attendees.
- Anyone attending the event can enter the costume contest
- You can not vote for yourself
- You can vote for up to 3 different contestants
- You can only cast max 1 vote on each contestant
- The vote is anonymous to everyone except the organizer who has to confirm that the vote is readable and that you didn't vote for yourself
- The winner is the contestant with the most votes
- In the event of a tie for first place, a second round of voting will be held between the tied contestants only
- In the event of a tie for any other place than first, all tied contestants will receive full points
- The received points are halved each subsequent place down, stopping at 1000 points.
- The voting criteria is entirely arbitrary and is up to each individual voter to make their reasoning.
