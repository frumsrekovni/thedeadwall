The Dead Wall 2025
Modified Riichi Mahjong

The tournament will be played with Modified WRC 2025 rules + Rulecards. The modifications of the WRC 2025 rules are as followed: Red fives, Nagashi Mangan(Counts as a win), Counted yakuman, +10 minutes to timer and Uma 30/10.

The rulecards is a modification of the Dreamland Odyssey mode released to Mahjong Souls. There are 3 decks. Dealer, Fortune and Curse. Dealer only affects the dealer, Fortune has positive effects and Curse has negative effects.

How a hanchan will be played: When the hanchan starts 1 card is drawn from each deck. All 3 cards will be placed in the middle of the table or wherever is agreed upon amongst the players. The drawn cards will apply for the entire round wind. At South 1, the existing cards are discarded and 3 new cards are drawn to replace them, one from each deck, and applies for the entire South round wind.

All rulecards:

Dealer deck:
Discipline: Dealer can only Ron when in Riichi or Open hand. Tsumo(Self-draw) is unaffected.
Long Night: When dealership continues honba(Continuance counter) increases by 5.
Forfeit: If the dealer is tenpai(one tile away from a complete hand) at exhaustive draw they get all the Riichi bets.
Speculation: Every time the dealer calls Pon or Kan, an extra Dora indicator will immediately be revealed (maximum 4 can be revealed).
Freebie: When the dealer wins with Ron, earns an additional 1000 points per Dora.


Fortune:
Shadow World: When player wins with Riichi, Normal Dora is ignored, but Ura Dora is doubled. (Red Five is unaffected.)
Open up: Iipeikou(Twin sequences) and Ryanpeikou(Double twin sequences) can be open.
Goldmine: Dora Indicators are also counted as Dora.
Super All Simples: Tanyao(All Simples) is counted as 3-Han.
Risktaker: Riichi is counted as 2-Han, Ippatsu(Unbroken) as 2-Han, Double Riichi as 4-Han.
Fortune:
Boast: No-ten Penalty and winning points for Nagashi Mangan(Mangan at Draw) are doubled.
Overpreparation: Declaring Riichi costs 2000 points.
Secret Deal: Your hand has to be atleast 3 han. Otherwise pay reverse mangan.
Toll of Love: When a player calls Chii, Pon, and Open Kan on a Dora tile another player discarded, they need to pay 2000 points.
