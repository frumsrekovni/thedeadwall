The Dead Wall 2025
Doracula's Riichi Mahjong

In the spirit of halloween and frightening luck, here is Doracula's riichi mahjong.

The rules:
The riichi mahjong games will be played with modified WRC 2025 rules + Rulecards(randomly drawn additional rules for each game). The modifications are as follows:

- Three red fives are used (1 for each suit).
- No head-bump. (Multiple Ron is allowed)
- Nagashi Mangan (流し満貫) is used. (Doesn't count as a win)
- Kokushi(Thirteen Orphans) can be won by robbing a concealed kan.
- Double yakuman is applied for: Single-wait Suuankou, Thirteen-wait Kokushi, True Nine Gates, and Four Big Winds.
- The Uma is: +30P / +10P / -10P / -30P, split in case of a tie.
- The time limit is 80 minutes. When the time ends, the players only finish their current hand.
- Live audience is allowed.
- 1 han special yaku: Bloody Mary - Hand entirely of manzu(characters) and Red dragon. Red dragon is not required. — courtesy of Anna

- Each player starts with 30,000 points.
- No abortive draw (途中流局): A hand only ends when someone wins or no more tiles are left to draw.
- The game only ends when it goes past South 4 or hit time limit.
- No bankruptcy: play continues with negative scores.
- Mangan rounding up: 4 han 30 fu and 3 han 60 fu are scored mangan.
- Kandora is flipped before the discard.
- It is allowed to declare riichi with less than four tiles left in the live wall.
- Liability payment applies for Big Dragons, Big Winds and Four Quads.
- A double wind pair is worth 2 fu.



Rulecards:
Every game will be played with rulecards. Rulecards are an additional random 3 rules that are added at East 1 and replaced in South 1 with 3 new random rulecards.

It's a modification of the Dreamland Odyssey mode released to Mahjong Souls. There are 3 decks. Dealer, Fortune and Curse. Dealer only affects the dealer, Fortune has positive effects and Curse has negative effects.

How a game of Doracula's riichi mahjong will play out:
There will be 3 separate deck of cards at your table. When the organizer starts the game timer 1 random card from each deck is drawn immediately and placed face up in the middle of the table or wherever is agreed upon amongst the 4 players. After that East 1 may start as normal.

When the game reaches South 1 the existing cards are discarded and 3 new cards are drawn, one from each deck, and applies until the game ends.

The organizer will announce when the game timer hits 80 minutes. That means that you finish your current hand and then end the game.

When the game ends you sum up all your score and subtract your starting points(30000), this number is written down on the provided scoresheet. Below this number you write down your UmaWinner of a game gets 30000 extra points, 2nd place gets 10000 extra points, 3rd place gets -10000 points and last place gets -30000 points. These points are what players often call "Uma" . The point sticks and rulecards are then reset to their starting position.

Lastly, the scoresheet is handed to the organizer.

All rulecards:
Dealer deck:
Discipline: Dealer can not dama(tenpai with a closed hand) ron. Can only Ron when in Riichi or Open hand. Tsumo(Self-draw) is unaffected.
Long Night: When dealership continues honba(Continuance counter) increases by 5.
Forfeit: If the dealer is tenpai at exhaustive draw they get the riichi deposit.
Speculation: Every time the dealer calls Pon or Kan, an extra Dora indicator will immediately be revealed (maximum 4 can be revealed).
Glass Cannon: When the dealer wins with Ron their hand gets an extra 1 han, but when hit by Ron that hand gets an extra 1 han.

Fortune:
Shadow World: When player wins with Riichi, Normal Dora is ignored, but Ura Dora is doubled. (Red Five is not affected)
Open up: Iipeikou(Twin sequences) and Ryanpeikou(Double twin sequences) can be open.
Goldmine: Dora/Uradora indicators are also counted as Dora.
Super All Simples: Tanyao(All Simples) is counted as 3-Han.
Risktaker: Riichi is counted as 2-Han, Ippatsu(Unbroken) as 2-Han, Double Riichi as 4-Han.

Curse:
Boast: Noten payments and winning points for Nagashi Mangan(流し満貫) are doubled.
Overpreparation: The riichi bet is 2000 points.
Slow down: Winning hand has to be 3 han or higher. If not, you win no points, pay everyone 4000 points each and the dealer rotates.
Toll of Love: When a player calls Chii, Pon, or Open Kan on a Dora, the discarder has to pay the caller 2000 points.
Hoarder: When hit by Ron pay an extra 1000 points per dora in your hand.
