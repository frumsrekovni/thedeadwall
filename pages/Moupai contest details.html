The Dead Wall 2025
Moupai contest details

Anyone attending the event can enter the moupai contest, including non-players. It is not mandatory. Best of 1 knockout tournament until the final two. The winner will get a prize and 10000 points counted towards the final score of the entire event. Second place gets 5000 points. The rest of the participants will receive 1000 points.

How it will be performed:
Each participant will be randomly called up to moupai a tile while looking into the eyes of Doracula. If you get it correct you survive that round. If you get it wrong you are eliminated. This continues until only two participants remain. The final two will be paired up and moupai simultaneously. The one who gets it correct while the other gets it wrong wins that round. This moupai final will be a best of 5.

Rules:
- If you break eye contact with Doracula while moupaiing you are eliminated.
- You need to announce your guess out loud before revealing your tile.
- If your moupai takes longer than 1 minute, a clock will be called and Doracula will start counting down from 10. If the count reaches 0 you are eliminated.
- You can only use your hands and fingers to moupai.
- All the tiles in a standard Riichi set will be used except the flowers and white dragon.
- Doracula will not cheat.
